A Love Affair With Ice Cream

Blue Giraffe Creamery is based near Alberta Beach but travels in and around the Edmonton area.

We purchased the trailer in the fall of 2014 and due to many unforeseen obstacles we were only operational for May and June of 2015. We are now fully operational and looking forward to the upcoming season.

Our signature dish is Ice Cream made to order on site with natural ingredients and frozen with Liquid Nitrogen. We offer a variety of flavours that can be found on our Menu page. We serve two flavours each day and rotate through our menu so the flavours change weekly. We also offer Lollywaffles, delicious and easy to eat waffle treats on a stick available in both sweet and savory flavours as well as baked mini donuts.

Adding to the cold side of the menu is Frozen Lemonade, Blue Raspberry Slushie, and Ice Cappucino.

See the Locations page for locations, days and times. We are also available for private functions.
