Book Us Any Time!

Blue Giraffe Creamery is available for private engagements: weddings, fund raisers, family reunions, sports tournaments, grand openings, etc. Call us to make your next event something special!

Ice Cream only - $500 for 100 servings of ice cream. Servings over 100 are $5 each. Prices are based on two flavours, if more flavours are required price may change. This includes a maximum of four hours on site, setup, and travel within 100 km. If additional time is required on site there will be an additional charge of $100 per hour.

Waffles only, Mini Donuts only, or a combination with ice cream - Please contact us and we would be happy to give you a price.

Additional cost for travel over 100km may apply.
